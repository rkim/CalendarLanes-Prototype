grouping layout using divs

Depending on analysis of the event-cells to be displayed (event-cell overlap, legibility, etc. via heuristics yet to be created), lanes may be automatically grouped by location (or possibly service or staff).

Scrolling near the scrollbar boundary will trigger an ajax call to the controller for additional event-cell data, and would result in rearrangement of the event-cell grid.
